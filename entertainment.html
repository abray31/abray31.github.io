Entertainment

Explore to your heart's content

Museums & Galleries

Taniti has a local history museum, as well as several art galleries that offer rich insight into the local culture.

Aquatic activities

Go on a chartered fishing tour, snorkel, jet-ski, swim, or just relax on pristine white sand beaches and soak up the sun.

Nightlife

Enjoy the various pubs, microbreweries, dance clubs, arcades, and movie theaters many tourists frequent in the evenings.

Ziplining

Interested in combining sightseeing with exploration and adventure? Ziplining may be for you! Enjoy our various courses that take you through the lush rainforest that spans across Taniti.

Golf

Beginning next year, Taniti will have it's first ever nine-hole golf course and we anticipate the course being a popular hit among our visitors.

Island Tours

Want a more in-depth perspective of the island? Enjoy a helicopter ride, boat, or bus tour featuring one of our expert guides.

Sightseeing

Curious about seeing Taniti up close? Enjoy hikes through the rainforest or scale our world-famous active volcano. Don't forget to also explore the various white-sand beaches that surround the island.
