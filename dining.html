Dining

You'll never go hungry while you're here

Fine dining and convenience

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine. Taniti also has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.

Tanitian Cuisine

Fish and rice are staples on Taniti thanks to the local fishing and agricultral industries, so many of the local restaurants offer a sampling of many tasteful dishes that feature these two main food groups. There are five different local restaurants to choose from to experience a taste of Taniti.

American Cuisine

Missing the taste of home? Have no fear. Many of our visitors arriving from the U.S. will find familiar foods at one of our three American-themed restaurants located in Taniti City.

Pan-Asian

In addition to its native cuisine and the well-known American style restaurants, Taniti also features two pan-Asian themed restaurants where you can explore many of the various foods common in neighboring countries.
